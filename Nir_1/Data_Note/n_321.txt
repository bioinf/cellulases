            We sequenced microbial DNA from an environmental sample using
            Illumina's sequencing technology and generated several dozen Gbp.
            Raw reads have been deposited already in the short read sequence
            archive. After assembling the raw data we selected 90 putative
            full-length genes containing a PFAM domain characteristic for
            particular carbohydrate active enzymes. We tested the recombinant
            proteins of these candidates for the predicted activity.
            The JGI and collaborators endorse the principles for the
            distribution and use of large scale sequencing data adopted by the
            larger genome sequencing community and urge users of this data to
            follow them. It is our intention to publish the work of this
            project in a timely fashion and we welcome collaborative
            interaction on the project and analysis.
